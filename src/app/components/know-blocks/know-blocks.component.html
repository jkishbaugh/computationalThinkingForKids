Getting To Know The Blocks

  1. Have Scratch Cat ask for your name and then repeat it.

  2. Have Scratch Cat ask for your name and then say Hello to the person entering the name.

  3. Get Scratch Cat to walk continously in a circle with one button click

  4. Get Scratch Cat to walk in a circle pause to say hello, then continue in the circle

  5. Get Scratch Cat to walk in a circle pause to say hello when the space bar is pressed, then stop circling

Using Clones

  1. Start Scratch Cat at the center of the screen and create a clone that moves 10 steps if it is less than 100 steps from the center

  2. Now make Scratch look like he is getting closer to you when the clone moves.

  3. Use the ghost effect to make Scratch Cat look like he is running very fast towards you.
