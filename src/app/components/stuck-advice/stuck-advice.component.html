I'm Stuck, Now What...

Before you ask for help you should...

  1. Make sure you can describe the thing that is confusing you.
    • What are you trying to do?
    • Where are you stuck?
    • What have you tried?
  2. Can you break the problem into smaller parts?
  3. Is there a simpler part of the problem you know how to make work? Try building from there.
  4. Explain how you are stuck, what you have tried, and what you are trying to do to a friend.
